  1. His writings were responsible for fractionalizing the Catholic Church and sparking the Protestant Reformation

  3. He protested successfully against the corrupt practice of indulgences
  4. He wrote the hugely influential 95 thesis
  5. He stood firm at the stance at the diet of worms
  6. Luther bible was widely read spreading its teaching to the common people
  7. He wrote the small and large cathechisms
  8. He is regarded as the pioneer of the protestant hymnody
  9. He made hymns an integral part of lutheranism
  10. He is one of the most influential figures in the history of christianity
